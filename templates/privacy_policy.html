Information Collection

To provide a seamless service experience, we collect specific data points during your interaction with our platform. This collection is limited to what is essential for operation.

  • Identity data including full name and professional designations.
  • Contact details such as verified email addresses and phone numbers.
  • Technical logs including IP addresses and browser configurations.
  • Secure encrypted authentication and protected server infrastructure.
  • Restricted administrative access to sensitive customer information.
  • Regular monitoring and security updates for platform protection.

Data Usage

The primary objective of data processing is to maintain system integrity and facilitate service fulfillment between users and verified vendors.

Access to sensitive user information is restricted only to authorized administrators and verified personnel responsible for maintaining platform functionality and customer service operations.

Internal Processing Only

We do not use your personal information for automated profiling or unauthorized marketing campaigns.

All user information is handled securely and accessed only for legitimate operational and customer support purposes.

Cookies & Tracking

Our platform utilizes essential cookies to maintain session persistence, improve platform security, and ensure a smooth browsing experience across authenticated areas of the system.

Analytical and performance-related cookies may be used to understand user interaction patterns, optimize loading speeds, and improve the overall usability of our services.

Certain cookies help remember user preferences such as language settings, login sessions, recently accessed pages, and dashboard configurations for a more personalized experience.

Tracking technologies are implemented responsibly and are never used for unauthorized advertising, invasive monitoring, or sharing sensitive personal information with unrelated third parties.

Users may manage browser cookie preferences through their device settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the availability of some platform features and authentication functionality.

  • Essential cookies required for secure login sessions and authentication.
  • Performance tracking used to improve platform speed and stability.
  • Preference cookies used to remember user settings and navigation history.
  • Optional analytics tools activated only with user consent where applicable.

Security & Protection

We employ enterprise-level encryption protocols (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit.

Access to sensitive information is strictly restricted to authorized personnel and monitored through internal security systems.

Regular security audits, server monitoring, and infrastructure updates are implemented to maintain platform integrity and prevent unauthorized access attempts.

User account credentials, payment activities, and sensitive booking information are processed through protected environments designed to minimize security vulnerabilities and operational risks.

  • Secure encrypted communication for all authenticated sessions.
  • Multi-layer infrastructure protection and server monitoring.
  • Restricted administrator access for sensitive customer data.
  • Continuous security updates and vulnerability management.

Vendor & Third Party Access

Information is shared with third-party vendors only when necessary to complete a requested service or operational requirement.

All vendors, partners, and service providers are legally obligated to comply with strict confidentiality agreements and applicable data protection standards.

Third-party access is limited to the minimum information required for service fulfillment, support coordination, booking management, or technical infrastructure operations.

We do not authorize vendors or external partners to independently market products or misuse customer information outside approved operational activities.

  • Limited vendor access for service assignment and operational support.
  • Confidential handling of customer-related service information.
  • Secure contractual agreements with external providers.
  • Monitoring and review of third-party data handling practices.

User Rights

Under global privacy regulations, users maintain control over personal information and account-related data.

Customers may review, update, or request modifications to stored information through account settings or by contacting platform support services.

Requests related to privacy, data access, and account management are processed according to applicable legal and operational requirements.

  • The right to request a complete export of personal account data.
  • The right to rectify inaccurate or outdated information.
  • The right to request permanent deletion of account records where applicable.
  • The right to review how personal information is processed and stored.

Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill service obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security records, or comply with legal requirements.

Inactive accounts may be archived after extended periods of inactivity in accordance with internal data management policies.

Archived information is protected through restricted access controls and may be permanently removed after applicable retention periods expire.

Certain transactional records, invoices, and operational logs may be retained longer where legally required for compliance or auditing purposes.

  • Secure archival procedures for inactive customer accounts.
  • Controlled retention of operational and transactional records.
  • Permanent deletion policies for expired archived data.
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory retention obligations.
